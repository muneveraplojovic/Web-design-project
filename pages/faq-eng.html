Why is it important to have a strong password?

By using a strong password, you make it harder for potential attackers to access your online accounts. A combination of different characters increases security and reduces the risk of hacking.

How to create a strong password?

Create a password that combines different letters, numbers, and special characters. Avoid obvious combinations such as birthdays or names. Using longer phrases or sentences can also enhance security.

What is two-factor authentication (2FA)?

Two-factor authentication is an additional layer of security that requires more than one way of confirming identity when logging into an account. This may include the use of SMS codes, authentication apps, or biometric verification, further protecting your account from unauthorized access.
