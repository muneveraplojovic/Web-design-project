TIPS

USING A STRONG PASSWORD

Creating a strong password is crucial for protecting your online accounts. A combination of letters, numbers, and special characters enhances the security of your password. Choose something that is hard to guess but easy to remember for you.

REGULAR PASSWORD CHANGES

To ensure the security of your online accounts, regularly change your passwords. Changing your password every few months prevents unauthorized access to your data and reduces the risk of hacking attacks.

2FA AUTHENTICATION

Enable multi-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible, as it adds an extra layer of security to your account. This may include using an SMS code, authentication app, or physical verification device.
